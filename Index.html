Movo - Time management device

How many times did you find yourself in a hurry?

We think that the classic way to measure time are not efficient enough when you are focused on doing something else and so, you lose the sense of time. Movo provides you a new way to organize your schedule in order to be more productive and efficient.

Differenty from a phone alarm clock is not possible to delay or switch the reminders off, movo itself is your visual reminder that keeps working telling you how much time is passing by and you need to get ready.

Movo is mainly composed by two parts: the app and the physical object.

With the app you can set 3 kind of reminders for each event that will be shown by the physical object as a visual and sound feedback.

The green light tells you that you have a task to accomplish that day, also the name of the task will be shown by a notification on your mobile. The red light means that you have to be quick and when the buzzer starts ringing you have no time anymore. You can push it and movo will start swinging and it turns off.

Year: 2018-2019
Course: Hardware & Software for Design
With: Erica Colombo, Alessandro Ceriani, Junpeng Zhao.
